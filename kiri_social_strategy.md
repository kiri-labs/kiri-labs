# Kiri Research Labs Social Media Strategy
## AI Model Compression Research Focus

### Content Pillars (6-Pillar Strategy)

#### 1. Research Breakthroughs (25%)
- Latest model compression techniques
- Research paper summaries
- Technical innovations
- Benchmark results

#### 2. Industry Insights (20%)
- AI industry trends
- Market analysis
- Competitive landscape
- Future predictions

#### 3. Behind-the-Scenes (15%)
- Lab culture
- Research process
- Team spotlights
- Daily research life

#### 4. Educational Content (20%)
- Explain compression concepts
- Tutorials and guides
- Technical explanations
- Best practices

#### 5. Community Engagement (15%)
- Industry discussions
- Conference highlights
- Collaborations
- Q&A sessions

#### 6. Thought Leadership (5%)
- Vision pieces
- Opinion articles
- Future of AI
- Ethical considerations

### Target Platforms & Frequency
- **Twitter/X**: 3-5 posts daily
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3 posts weekly
- **Instagram**: 3-4 posts weekly
- **Blog**: 1-2 posts weekly

### Key Messaging Themes
1. Making AI more efficient and accessible
2. Cutting-edge research with real-world impact
3. Democratizing AI through optimization
4. Sustainable AI development
5. Bridging research and industry

### Content Calendar Structure
- Monday: Research focus
- Tuesday: Industry insights
- Wednesday: Educational content
- Thursday: Behind-the-scenes
- Friday: Community engagement
- Weekend: Thought leadership
