# Contributing to Kiri Research Labs

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Kiri Research Labs. We are building the efficiency frontier of AI, focusing on **TinyML**, **Edge Intelligence**, and **African Linguistics**.

## 🧪 Areas for Contribution

We welcome research and engineering contributions in our three core vectors:

### 1. 🧩 TinyML & SLMs
- Optimization of Small Language Models (0.5B - 3B).
- Techniques for quantization, pruning, and knowledge distillation.

### 2. 🏗️ Hierarchical Architectures
- Development of "Manager-Worker" cognitive topologies.
- Routing networks for decomposing complex tasks.

### 3. 🗣️ Naija Voice & Linguistics
- Datasets for tonal languages (Igbo, Yoruba, Pidgin).
- Adaptive tokenizers that ensure cultural nuance.

---

## 🚀 How to Submit

1.  **Fork** the repository.
2.  **Create a Branch** for your contribution.
3.  **Commit** your changes with clear, descriptive messages.
4.  **Push** to your fork.
5.  **Open a Pull Request (PR)** to our `main` branch.

Please ensure all data contributions adhere to ethical AI standards and respect user privacy.

**Intelligence is scalable. Let's build it together.**
